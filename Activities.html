Dago Methodist Child Development Center is recognized as the most renowed and popular organization among the others for its activeness and much effort yielding greater results and more productive resourcces.



We provide an age-graded curriculum for each church to contextualize for the needs of the children in its community. The curriculum is based on the outcomes we hope to see in the four facets of child development: spiritual, physical, cognitive and socio-emotional. We’re successful when the children in the Child Sponsorship Program:

  • commit their lives to Christ,
  • choose good health practices,
  • are physically healthy, are motivated to learn new skills,
  • demonstrate the skills to support themselves in the future
  • and interact with others in healthy and compassionate ways.
    • Bible Studies And Spiritual Formation

There is the involvement of the leaders of the church particularly the society pastors who empower the children and direct them to live worth live pleasing God.

    Youth Guidance And Monitoring Programs

    In order to put these children at the right posision, we organize guidance and monitoring programs to shape their attitudes in all ways:spiritually, physically and socially.

    We do these by inviting prominent people from various angles of life to have a speech with them and provide guidance.Such people include doctors and nurses, teachers, pastors.

    Sports & Games

    The organization also have special and qualified teachers who train them for sports activities like the volleyball, baseball, netball, athletics, and football.

    Our team participates in inter-organizational competitions at various stages. With our performance, we are always the best or with the best. Our team won the trophy for under 12 boys soccer the past season.

    Educational Programs

    With educational programs,we have qualified volunteers and teachers who dedicate their time to teach our children to help them attain better education.

    We organize quizes indoors and also participate in inter-organizational debates. We also embark on tours to some eductional institutions like the senior hihg schools and universities to learn, this gets the beneficiaries more inspired in education.Four of our beneficiaries wrote the recent Basic Educational Certicicate Examination(BECE) and the best student was chosen to be one of our own.
